WHAT ARE WE ALL ABOUT!

WineMapper is a web application that allows wine-lovers to have a wounderful experience in
Macedonia. The app leads its users to the best wineries locally by showcasing precise location of every
winery in Macedonia, showcasing the nearest winery and finding out about new winieries in general.
With the help of OpenStreetMap, our users can easily plan their next trip and even share their
experience by writing a review or rating the given winery. The main goal is to encourage wine-lovers
to visit the wide range of domestic wineries, as well as simplified navigation that will allow the users to
visit every winery in Macedonia.
